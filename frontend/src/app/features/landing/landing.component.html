The overlooked gap

Closed is not the same as effective.

The blind spot lives in the time between execution and outcome. Regrowth, a missed subsection, a failed photo — none of it shows up in a “completed” checkbox.

🗂️

What today records

“Work order closed.” An activity happened, somewhere, at some time. Evidence may be partial and nobody is blocked.

🎯

What actually matters

Was clearance restored? Did compatible cover establish? Is the span still safe at fire season — not just on the day of work?

⚖️

Why it’s not optional

Clearance is the outcome regulators check (NERC FAC-003; state wildfire-mitigation plans). Auditing exists precisely because “done” ≠ “effective”.
