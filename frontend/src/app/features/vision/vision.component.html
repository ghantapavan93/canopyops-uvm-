The boundary is the product

Augmentation, never autonomy.

AI may

  • Rank spans by risk
  • Classify & cluster imagery
  • Predict species-specific regrowth
  • Explain a ranking in plain language
  • Summarize an audit trail · pre-fill a form

AI may never

  • Authorize a cut
  • Prescribe a herbicide or rate
  • Declare a site effective, safe, or compliant
  • Approve a closure
  • Trigger a de-energization

Every output shows its evidence, carries a confidence, and is reversible — signed by a certified human.
